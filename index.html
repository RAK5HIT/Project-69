places must visit

prithvi cafe

i had visited in dec 2018

Quaint little restraunt tucked inside a by-lane of Juhu. Blink and you will miss the entrance to the cafe. So be alert enough to spot the black signboard that says "Prithvi Cafe" while you are on the main road. The restraunt has a reasonably good menu at reasonably good prices. The food is delicious, the serving are quite large and can be shared by 2 people. Attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebrities on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid dissapointments. One other days, the crowd is pretty hyped and happening. Hosts the famous "Prithvi theater feast", which you mst attend if possible.

Jantar mantar

i had visited in fed 2019

It is a wonerful place. Must visit this place. Most haired tour guide so it will be better to understand all the things. It is a beautifully maintained small complex with yellow painted walls It's a historical site famous among tourists =. It contains a number of different devices that were used to measure time in different eras in the Indian history. It has a nominal ticket price of 15 rs but its Rs. 225-250 for foreigners. Nice place to visit. We had an incredible experience visiting astronomical instruments built around 300 years ago



yiga choeling monastery

ihad visited in oct 2019

One of the oldest Monasteries in Darjeling. It is situated in a peaceful and beautiful landscape within 15 minutes walk from groom railway Station. We can see religious God and Goddess' paintings believed by buddhism inside which can give spiritual power to the one who prays there with a clean heart. From the ompund of Monastery on a clear day one can have a scenery of hills all around which is great for nature lovers.

  • LEGO plans to find replacement material for bricks by 2030
